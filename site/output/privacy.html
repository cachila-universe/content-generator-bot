Last updated: March 08, 2026

Introduction

TechLife Insights ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at https://tech-life-insights.com.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze website traffic (via Google Analytics)
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Remember user preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some website functionality.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our content and website experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns and user behavior
  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics — Website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Display advertising. Google Ads Policy

Some articles contain affiliate links. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps support the creation of free content on this website.

We only recommend products and services we believe provide value. Our editorial content is not influenced by affiliate partnerships.

Data Retention

We retain automatically collected data for up to 26 months via Google Analytics. We do not store personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for website operations.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of data collection (via browser settings or opt-out tools)
  • Request information about how your data is used

For EU/EEA residents: You have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For California residents: You have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
